Nevertheless, your Personal Data (as defined below) will always be managed in accordance with the Information Notice in force at the time of collection and as further detailed in the updated versions of this Notice.     

  1. Personal Data collected     

The Company may collect Personal Data such as your name, surname, email address, phone number, home address, personal numeric code etc. (“Personal Data”) for the purpose of concluding the rental agreement and other documents required by the legislation in force, as well as for performing and invoicing the services provided by the Company to you. The processed categories of data include:      

  1. General Data: name, surname, personal numeric code, ID card / passport series and number and other personal data included in the copy of the identity document submitted to the Company, as well as the personal data contained in the copy of the driving licence;      

  2. Data regarding the status of the Data Subject: status as individual customer, representative of a legal entity or contact person of a legal entity;      

  3. Contact and billing data: your email address, phone number, account settings on the Company’s website and the data contained in that account, bank details such as the bank issuing your card, IBAN and other data visible on the bank card;      

  4. Reservation data: information on the start and end of the reservation, the location where the vehicle should be delivered and / or the pickup location;     

  5. Personal data resulting from communications with the Company: messages sent through the Company’s public communication platforms, letters or any other communications carried out via the Company’s platform, email or phone number that may contain personal data;      

  6. Photos and / or audio-video recordings: photos or audio-video recordings that may include your personal data (voice or image) as a result of recording the vehicle when it is handed over to you by the Company;     

  7. Statistical and marketing data: IP address, satisfaction level regarding the Company’s services, marketing preferences, surveys, marketing consent, data resulting from the non-essential cookies accepted by you, such as the categories of websites visited, location data and other similar information detailed in the Cookie Policy;      

As a rule, the personal data processed are collected directly from you. However, we may occasionally rely on third-party services to obtain such information, for example from public sources or from records made available by various authorities or public institutions.     

  1. When we collect personal data     

We collect your personal data when:     

  1. you are interested in our rental services and contact us through one of the available channels (email, phone, visiting our offices, website);      

  2. you are a representative or contact person of a legal entity that intends to or already engages in commercial relations with the Company;      

  3. you use our vehicle rental services;     

  4. you visit or browse our website or are redirected to our website following an advertisement or any other link to the Company’s website;     

  5. you create a user account on our website;     

  6. you contact us through various channels or request information about our services, including through social media communication channels such as Facebook, Instagram, etc.;     

  7. you subscribe to marketing communications (Newsletter), alerts or other services provided by us;     

We use cookies (small text files stored in your browser) and other specific techniques. When you visit the Website, we automatically collect data via your browser or device, through the use of cookies and similar technologies. For more details and to learn how to opt out, please see the Cookies section on our website  www.dacars.ro .     

  1. Purposes and legal grounds of processing     

The personal data categories listed above are processed based on the following legal grounds and purposes:      

  1. Legal ground: performance of the contract: ensuring interactions when you are interested in our services (pre-contractual steps prior to concluding the rental agreement) and for the execution of the rental contract;     

  2. Legal ground: performance of the contract or legitimate interest: ensuring interactions and establishing a relationship with you when we have a contractual relationship or a legitimate interest;     

  3. Legal ground: performance of the contract: ensuring communication and establishing a relationship with the entity you represent, whether you are the legal representative or the contact person for a current or potential corporate customer with whom we intend to conclude or have concluded a contract;     

  4. Legal ground: legal obligation: interacting with the competent authorities in case of traffic incidents, accounting and tax requirements, reporting, implementing know-your-customer measures for the prevention and combating of money laundering, fulfilling activities related to inspections and information requests from authorities such as the National Agency for Fiscal Administration (ANAF), the National Authority for Consumer Protection (ANPC), the Romanian Data Protection Authority (ANSPDCP), the Romanian Police and any other legal obligations imposed by public authorities and institutions;     

  5. Legal ground: legitimate interest: creating and managing the user account, handling vehicle rental requests placed through the user account;      

  6. Legal ground: legitimate interest: establishing, exercising or defending a legal claim, protecting the Company’s assets and values and those of the customers, improving the services provided;     

  7. Legal ground: legitimate interest: processing personal data that may result from photos / video recordings made of the vehicle in order to assess its condition at pickup and return;      

  8. Legal ground: consent: for marketing purposes insofar as you have agreed to such purposes, generally covering:     

  • providing information on commercial offers;     

  • providing information and invitations regarding the Company’s events;     

  • participation in surveys regarding the Company’s services and customer satisfaction;     

  • participation in market analyses and studies concerning the car rental market dynamics;     

  • participation in questionnaires following events organized by the Company;     

  • informing you by email or phone that you can leave online reviews or testimonials in order to recommend the services offered by the Company.     

If you no longer wish to receive marketing communications from us, please update your preferences by:     

  • changing the settings in your customer account;     

  • accessing the unsubscribe link available on the website;     

  • clicking the unsubscribe link displayed in the messages you receive from us;      

  • contacting the Company using the communication channels described below.     

If you opt out of marketing communications, we may still send you messages related to your orders or regarding updates to our policies, messages necessary under an ongoing contract with you.     

We also process personal data when the processing is necessary for the performance of a task carried out in the public interest.     

You also have the right to withdraw your consent at any time for any of the processing activities based on consent mentioned above.

The recipients of your Personal Data are the Company’s employees from the sales department and the departments responsible for managing the execution of rental contracts, from the accounting department, as well as services connected to the aforementioned contract, namely external Emergency Situations / Fire Safety services, external Accounting services, the Company’s external legal advisers or similar services, as well as public institutions.      

Your Personal Data will not be sold, shared or otherwise distributed to third parties without your consent, unless we are required to do so under applicable law, a court order or a government regulation, or if such disclosure is otherwise necessary to achieve the purposes described above, to support any criminal or other investigation or legal proceedings in Romania or abroad.

  • Legal or administrative authorities: as required by the applicable laws;     

In any case, the Company will require such third parties to:

  1. Transfer of Personal Data     

The Company will not transfer Personal Data to other jurisdictions, to countries that cannot be considered to provide the same level of protection as the jurisdiction in which you are located, including outside or within the European Economic Area. If the Company needs to transfer Personal Data within the European Economic Area, it will ensure that appropriate safeguards are implemented, as required by the applicable data protection legislation.     

  1. Retention period of Personal Data     

Personal Data will be stored for a limited period, as long as the rental agreement is in force, as long as it is necessary to perform the contract, during the resolution of any dispute, and in accordance with applicable legislation for as long as we are legally required to keep contracts and other legal documents in the Company’s accounting records and archive.     

In general, your data are retained: (i) for the duration of the rental agreement, (ii) for the lifetime of the user account until its deletion, (iii) for the time needed to provide the requested information, (iv) for the duration of any investigation or administrative procedure related to the performance of the contract by state authorities and (v) for the duration of any statutory retention period or for the Company’s legitimate interests.      

Audio-video recordings made when the vehicle is handed over to you will be kept for 30 days from the end of the rental contract.      

Marketing consent will be retained for 2 years from the date when the explicit consent was obtained.      

Copies of your ID card and driving licence will be stored for 30 days if you cancel the reservation, and for 1 year from the return of the rented vehicle upon termination of the contract.      

The Company has established technical and organisational measures to protect and ensure the confidentiality, integrity and availability of your processed Personal Data; we will prevent unauthorised use or access and prevent personal data breaches, in accordance with the applicable legislation.
